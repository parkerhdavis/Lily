# The Questionnaire

The questionnaire is a structured intake form that walks you through gathering client information section by section. Instead of filling in variables one at a time across different documents, the questionnaire lets you collect everything in one place.

---


## Accessing the Questionnaire

From the **Client Hub**, click the **Questionnaire** card at the top of the document list. The card shows your progress — how many fields have been filled out of the total.

---


## How It Works

The questionnaire is organized into **tabs** (e.g., "Client Info," "Client Contacts," "Assignments & Decisions"). Each tab contains collapsible **sections** with related questions that map directly to template variables.

### Tabs

Tabs group sections by topic. Click a tab to switch between areas of the questionnaire. The tab structure is defined by the active questionnaire definition and can be customized from the [Questionnaire Editor](wiki/05_Pipeline/04.01_Managing%20Questionnaires.md).

### Question Types

There are three types of questions:

**Text questions** — Standard text fields that map to replacement variables. Type your answer and it's stored as the variable value.

**Conditional toggles** — True/false switches that map to conditional variables. These control which version of text appears in the final document (e.g., "Is Client Single?" determines whether the document uses single-person or couple language).

**Contact-role pickers** — Dropdowns that let you assign a contact to a role. Select from your existing contacts, or choose "Other" to enter values manually. See [Contacts](wiki/02_Client_Management/01.02_Contacts.md) for more on how contact roles work.

### Section Notes

Each section supports two types of notes:

- **Client notes** — Visible to the client; useful for explanations or instructions
- **Internal notes** — For the legal team only; useful for reminders or case-specific context

---


## Progress Tracking

The questionnaire tracks how many text fields have been filled in:

- The **Questionnaire card** on the Client Hub shows progress (e.g., "12 / 35")
- The **questionnaire header** shows the same count while you're working
- Each **section header** shows per-section completion stats

This count only tracks text questions — conditional toggles and contact-role pickers aren't included since they always have a value (defaulting to unchecked or unassigned).

---


## Searching

If the questionnaire is long, use the search field at the top to filter questions (`Ctrl+F` to focus). Matching text is highlighted in the results, and sections that don't contain matches are hidden.

---


## Questionnaire Versioning

Each questionnaire is based on a **questionnaire definition** — a structure that specifies which tabs, sections, and questions appear. When you open the questionnaire for a client, the `.lily` file records which definition (and version) was used, so there's a clear record of which intake form was current at the time.

Questionnaire definitions are managed from the [Pipeline](wiki/05_Pipeline/04.01_Managing%20Questionnaires.md).
